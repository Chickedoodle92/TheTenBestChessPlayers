The Ten Best Chess Player

#10 Alexander Alekhine

Alexander Alekhine was born in Moscow and lived to 1892 to 1946. He was one of the first grand-master to be recognizes and he held the world champion title from 1927-1935 and then to 1937-1945. He also created the famous Alekhine Defense.

#9 Mikhail Tal

Mikhail Tal lived from 1936 till 1992. He was youngest world champion at the time being 23 year old and gained the nicknamed “The Magician from Riga”. He learned chess when he was six and he has a effective and unique style of playing.

#8 Emmanuel Lasker

Emmanuel Lasker was German and lived to 1868 till 1941. He was the 2nd official world champion, and he held the title through 1894 and all the way to 1921. The longest of any world chess champion. He also wrote the famous book “Common Sense in Chess”

#7 Vladimir Kramnik

He was the top player for over 25 years and the world champion from 2000-2007. He was born on 1975 and recently retired in 2019. He also dethrone the famous Kasparov.
