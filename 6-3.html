The Ten Best Chess Player

#6 Mikhail Botvinnik

Mikhail Botvinnik lived from 1911 till 1995 and retired by 1970. He was known for his flexible playing style and he was the top player for over 30 years. He also was the 6th world champion.

#5 Anatoly Karpov

Anatoly Karpov claimed the world champion title between 1975 and 1985. He was the 12th world champion with a very well-rounded playing style. He was born on 1951 and wrote many famous chess books.

#4 Jose Raul Capablanca

Jose Raul Capablanca had 40 wins between 1916 to 1924 and went 8 years without a single loss He beat the famous chess player EmmauneL Lasker in 1921 and he specialized in the end game of chess.

3# Bobby Fischer

Bobby Fischer is the only American world champion. Throughout 1970 and 1971 he won 20 consecutive games and became the world champion in 1972. He held onto this till 1975 and had the "Game of the Century"
