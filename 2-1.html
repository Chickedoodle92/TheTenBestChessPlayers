The Ten Best Chess Player

#2 Magnus Carlsen

Magnus Carlsen has been the number one ranked player since 2013 and has held onto it till recently. He was the youngest player to pass 2800 ELO and reached a peaked ELO of 2889. He held the world champion in blitz, rapid, and standard in addition to a 125 undefeated streak.

#1 Garry Kasparov

He is the youngest world champion at 22 years old and reached a peak ELO of 2856. He retired in 2005 and trained two famous grand master, Mangus Carlsen and Hikrua Nakamura. He held the world champion title from 1985 to 2000.
